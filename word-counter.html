Free Word Counter Tool for Writers, Students, and SEO Teams

This free online word counter helps you measure content length and readability before publishing. Whether you are writing blog posts, social captions, product descriptions, outreach emails, or academic assignments, you can check your word count and character count in real time without installing anything.

Unlike basic counters, this tool also shows paragraph count, sentence count, reading time, and speaking time. These metrics are useful when optimizing on-page SEO, matching client word limits, and preparing scripts for voice or video content. The tool runs directly in your browser, so your text stays private.

How to Use the Word Counter

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Review live metrics for words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.
  3. Use reading time and speaking time to estimate user engagement.
  4. Refine your copy and re-check instantly until the text meets your target.

Best Use Cases

  • Blog writers targeting ideal article length for search intent.
  • Students checking assignment limits before submission.
  • SEO teams optimizing metadata, snippets, and body copy.
  • Copywriters editing ad or landing-page text for clarity.
