Free Image Compressor — Reduce File Size Without Quality Loss

This free browser-based image compressor helps you reduce the size of JPEG, PNG, and WebP images instantly, with no upload to any server. Smaller images load faster, improve your Core Web Vitals scores, and help your pages rank higher on Google.

Large images are the leading cause of slow page load times. Compressing your images before publishing them to a website, blog, or e-commerce store is one of the easiest performance wins available. This tool lets you compare the original and compressed versions side by side and download the result immediately.

How to Compress Images

  1. Drag and drop or select your image file (JPG, PNG, WebP).
  2. Adjust the quality slider to your desired compression level.
  3. Preview the compressed image and check the file size reduction.
  4. Download the compressed image with one click.

Image Compression FAQ

Does compression reduce image quality? Lossy compression (used for JPEGs) reduces quality slightly at lower settings. Lossless compression keeps quality identical while removing unnecessary metadata.

What formats are supported? JPEG, PNG, and WebP images are supported. The compressed output can be downloaded as JPEG or PNG.

Is my image uploaded to a server? No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

Is this tool free? Yes, completely free with no signup required.
