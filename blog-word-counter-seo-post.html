Word Counter vs Character Counter

While both metrics are important, they serve different purposes:

Word Count: Essential for SEO, content planning, and meeting writing requirements. Most blog posts aim for 300-1500 words.

Character Count: Critical for social media posts with strict limits. Twitter/X allows 280 characters, LinkedIn 1300 characters.

Our word count checker provides both metrics simultaneously, helping you optimize content for different platforms and purposes.
